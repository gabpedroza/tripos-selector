How it works (FSRS Algorithm)

This application uses the Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler (FSRS) algorithm to optimize your study schedule. Instead of reviewing everything randomly, the system prioritizes topics you are likely to forget.

  • Topics: The system tracks your mastery of specific topics (e.g., "Plastic theory").
  • Ratings:
    • Again (1): Complete blackout. You need to review this topic very soon.
    • Hard (2): You solved it, but it took significant effort or you made minor errors.
    • Good (3): Solved correctly with a normal amount of effort.
    • Easy (4): Solved quickly and effortlessly.
  • Scheduling: Based on your ratings, the algorithm calculates the optimal "Next Review Date" for each topic. When you start a session, it picks due topics first.
